Organization Switcher

The CenturyLink Platform supports the ability to create sub-orgs, which fall under a parent org. Sub orgs have of the advantage of:

  • Hierarchy: user permissions flow down, but not up or sideways
  • Billing: sub accounts may be billed separately or to the parent account
  • Share Parent Networks: sub accounts may access their parent account's networks, in addition to their own network
  • Inheritance: account settings are inherited

If a user has access to multiple orgs, their org context needs to be maintained as they navigate to different platform satelite apps. This component is a means for platform customers to view their current org context, change org context, and maintain that context across different platform satelite apps.
